Final Project! Cat Scheduler

A clock with time-based prompts that remind cat-owners when and what to do to take care of their feline friends! There are prompts such as breakfast time at 8am, taking your cat for a walk at 4pm, and cuddling with your cat at 6pm.

In this gif demo, you can see the RTC outputting real-time onto the LCD (seconds are changing)
